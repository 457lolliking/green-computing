The Crisis in Numbers

E-waste is the fastest growing waste stream globally. Here's what it means for us.

62 Million Tons

Global e-waste generated in 2025. Projected to hit 75M by 2030 if nothing changes.

17.4% Recycled

Only a fraction is properly recycled. The rest is dumped, burned, or lost in homes.

$62 Billion

Value of raw materials like gold, copper trashed in e-waste yearly. We literally throw away money.
