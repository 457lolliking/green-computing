The E-Waste Problem

Discarded electronics with batteries or plugs. Toxic if mishandled, valuable if recycled.

What's Inside?

Toxic: Lead, mercury, cadmium, brominated flame retardants.
Valuable: Gold, silver, copper, palladium. 1 ton of phones has 100x more gold than 1 ton of ore.

Cameroon Context

We import 2,000+ tons of used electronics yearly. Much ends up in dumpsites like Mussaka. Informal burning releases dioxins - linked to cancer and birth defects.

Health Impact

Lead poisoning affects IQ in children. Mercury from batteries contaminates water. One phone battery can pollute 60,000L of water.
