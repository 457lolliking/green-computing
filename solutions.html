E-Waste Solutions: The 4 R's

Refuse

Do you need that upgrade? Marketing drives 30% of e-waste. Buy only if necessary.

Reduce

Extend device life. Replace batteries, add RAM, use cloud storage to avoid new HDDs.

Reuse

Donate to UB students, sell on Jumia, convert old phones to CCTV/security cams.

Recycle

Never trash electronics. Use certified centers only - they safely extract materials.
