What is Green Computing?

The practice of using computing resources efficiently and responsibly to reduce environmental impact.

1. Energy Efficiency

Data centers use 2% of global electricity. Efficient code, sleep modes, and low-power chips cut this drastically. A laptop on sleep uses 2W vs 60W active.

2. Sustainable Design

Modular phones like Fairphone, laptops with replaceable RAM/SSD. Designing for repair, not disposal.

3. Lifecycle Thinking

80% of a laptop's carbon footprint comes from manufacturing. Using it 2 years longer cuts lifetime emissions by 30%.
